Courses We Offer

CSE

Computer Science and Engineering was established in the year 1994. The primary goal of CSE is to provide best IT infrastructure, world class learning & research environment, adopt industry practices through industry collaborations and inculcate moral and ethical values

ECE

The Department was established in 1970 with B.E. programme in Electronics and Communication Engineering. Later on, in 1981 post graduate programme in M.E. Applied Electronics and in 2003 M.E.

IT

The Department of Information Technology was established in the year 1999. Our Mission is to provide quality education to the students and impart IT excellence in them. Apart from making them good technocrats, we also provide individual attention to make them good citizens of our nation to serve the industry and society constructively.

About Us

HOGWARTS is well-known for its renowned educational practices, which has been recognized and endowed with several awards. The founders of the Institution, Col. Prof. Dumbeldore Chancellor & Founder President and Dr. Severous Snape President believes in Education for All despite their financial means which will promote and uplift the society. In accord to their vision, Hogwarts Scholarship scheme was started and has supported students since the inception.
